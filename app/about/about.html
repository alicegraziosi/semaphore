General Info

{{appName}} - {{subtitle}} - is an interactive web-based application based on an ongoing research on Linked Open Data customisable visualisation.

The purpose of this research is to create an environment that permits users to make customizable visualizations of LOD dataset, facilitating the access to semantic-enriched information for non-expert users and hiding the complexity of the underlying ontological model.
